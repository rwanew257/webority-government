Frequently Asked Questions

Webority Technologies provides AI-powered analytics platforms, RAG pipeline-based policy intelligence tools, NLP chatbots in 12+ Indian languages, document intelligence systems, fraud detection platforms, and scheme monitoring dashboards for India’s public sector. All AI systems are deployed within DPDP Act 2023-compliant, NIC-compatible architectures aligned with MeitY’s responsible AI guidelines. Proven at Bureau of Energy Efficiency’s Market Surveillance programme and Ministry of Tribal Affairs’ Sickle Scan platform.

Yes. All AI systems and data analytics platforms built by Webority Technologies for government clients are deployed on NIC National Data Centres or MeghRaj GI Cloud — 100% within India. This satisfies DPDP Act 2023 data localisation requirements and GoI data sovereignty policy. No government data transits through foreign servers at any point in the pipeline.

RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) combines a large language model with a searchable knowledge base — enabling AI to answer using only verified, department-approved data rather than general internet training data. For government, this means dashboards and policy intelligence tools that cite only official data sources, are explainable for audit purposes, and comply with MeitY’s AI ethical framework. Webority deploys RAG pipelines using LangChain and AutoGen within secure, NIC-compatible infrastructure.
