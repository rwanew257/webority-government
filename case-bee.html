Project Overview

Digitising India’s National Appliance Compliance Programme

The Bureau of Energy Efficiency’s Market Surveillance programme monitors compliance of energy-efficient appliances across India — from refrigerators and ACs to LED lights — ensuring manufacturers meet the prescribed Star Label standards.

Before this platform, all inspection workflows were manual — paper-based field reports, manual aggregation, and significant delays in compliance data reaching BEE’s central team. We replaced this entirely with a digitised, real-time platform spanning every State Designated Agency (SDA) in India.

What We Delivered

The platform was built around six integrated components — mobile applications serving field officers, dashboards serving BEE and the State Designated Agencies, and a registry connecting every actor in the regulatory chain on a single digital backbone.

  1. 01.

    Field Officer Mobile Application

    Android app for market surveillance officers — offline-first with local data storage, syncing inspection records when connectivity is available. Built for operation in remote retail environments.

  2. 02.

    Central Compliance Dashboard

    Real-time web dashboard for BEE and SDA administrators — showing inspection status, product compliance rates, geographic coverage, and officer activity across all states.

  3. 03.

    Inspection Workflow Digitisation

    End-to-end digitisation of the inspection process — from outlet selection and product sampling to test reports, compliance certificates, and enforcement actions.

  4. 04.

    SDA Integration & Multi-State Rollout

    Onboarding and integration of all 36+ State Designated Agencies — each with their own user management, jurisdiction-based data views, and compliance reporting.

  5. 05.

    Product & Manufacturer Database

    Centralised registry of all Star Labelled products and manufacturers — linked to inspection records, enabling automated compliance tracking and violation flagging.

  6. 06.

    Automated Reporting Engine

    Scheduled compliance reports for ministry leadership, state officials, and manufacturer communication — eliminating manual report preparation entirely.
