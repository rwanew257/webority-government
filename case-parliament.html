Project Overview

Digitising Operations Inside India’s Parliament

The Sansad Cafeteria Management System is a comprehensive digital platform deployed across multiple cafeteria outlets within Parliament premises. The platform manages every aspect of food service operations — from daily demand forecasting linked to Parliamentary sessions to multi-outlet billing, inventory management, and financial reconciliation.

Given the deployment environment — one of India’s most high-security premises — the platform required stringent access controls, audit logging, and compliance with Parliamentary security protocols throughout the development and deployment process.

Scope of Work

What We Delivered

  1. Multi-Outlet Operations Platform
    Unified digital management system for all cafeteria outlets within Parliament premises — centralised dashboard with outlet-level control and reporting.
  2. Session-Linked Demand Management
    Automated demand forecasting and menu planning linked to Parliamentary session schedules — ensuring accurate provisioning based on attendance and sitting days.
  3. Role-Based Access Control
    Multi-tiered access system for cafeteria managers, outlet staff, billing personnel, and administrative oversight — with complete audit trails on every action.
  4. Billing & POS Integration
    Digital billing across all outlets with POS terminal integration, token management, and real-time transaction tracking.
  5. Inventory & Procurement Management
    End-to-end inventory tracking, stock alerts, supplier management, and purchase order workflows aligned with government procurement rules.
  6. Financial Reporting & Reconciliation
    Automated daily, weekly, and monthly financial reports with outlet-wise P&L, collection reconciliation, and audit-ready documentation.
