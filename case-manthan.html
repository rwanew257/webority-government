The Mandate

One Secure Platform for a Distributed Defence Workforce

A Government of India Public Sector Undertaking under the Ministry of Defence, engaged in the manufacture of ammunition and ordnance, runs its operations across 17 units. Holding the organisation together depends on how reliably information moves between headquarters and the floor of each unit.

That movement ran on paper. Announcements travelled by notice board and email, while compliance checklists, grievances, incidents, ideas, and suggestions were collected on forms with no auditable trail and no real-time view for leadership. Webority Technologies engineered Manthan to bring every employee, voice, and workflow onto one secure platform, accessible from any phone in Hindi or English.

Scope of Work

What We Delivered

The platform was built around five integrated capabilities, spanning a mobile app for every employee and a role-based web portal for management. Together they bring communication, compliance, learning, and HR onto a single secure backbone connecting all 17 units.

  1. 01.

    Employee Mobile App

    Bilingual iOS and Android app with biometric and PIN unlock and OTP login. A daily feed of announcements and recognitions, quick links, and the holiday and training calendar, giving every employee the same single point of access regardless of role.

  2. 02.

    Engagement & Communication

    Targeted announcements from headquarters and units, public and unit-specific discussion forums, direct messaging between colleagues, and workplace surveys including anonymous ones. Built to strengthen transparency and collaboration across every unit.

  3. 03.

    Submissions & Compliance

    One Submissions hub for compliance checklists with photo evidence, ideas, suggestions, incident reports, and grievances. Every item is categorised, routed to the right handler, tracked through its lifecycle, and stored against the employee record.

  4. 04.

    Learning, Knowledge & HR Self-Service

    An e-learning library with completion tracking, version-controlled policy documents, and a digital e-Patrika magazine, alongside employee self-service for weekly attendance, leave balances, and monthly salary summaries.

  5. 05.

    Web Portal with Role-Based Access

    A web portal for the seven administrative roles, from Super Admin and Director through Unit Admin, Section Head, Approver, and Verifier to Content Creator. Employees use the mobile app only. Master data, branding, announcements, calendars, and content are managed with unit-scoped permissions and full audit logging on every action.

One Secure System of Record

Manthan replaced notice boards, email chains, and paper forms with a single platform. Announcements now reach exactly the right audience, every submission carries a complete audit trail, and leadership sees engagement and operations across all 17 units in real time.
