Capabilities

What We Build for Government

Every e-Governance platform we ship carries the same foundations — security, accessibility, interoperability and resilience — baked in from day one, not retrofitted before audit.

Role-Based Access & Audit Trails

Granular, role-scoped permissions with full audit logging on every action — so every approval, edit and submission is traceable to a user and a timestamp.

Multilingual & GIGW 3.0 Compliant

Built against GIGW 3.0 and WCAG 2.1 AA from day one, with multilingual interfaces so citizens are served in their own language across devices.

Offline-First & Low-Bandwidth

Field-ready applications that capture and sync data on patchy or absent connectivity — designed for low-bandwidth Bharat, not just metro broadband.

India Stack Integration

Aadhaar e-KYC, DigiLocker and UPI integrated against the published India Stack APIs under the relevant agreements and MeitY security guidelines.

NIC & API Compatible

Open-standard, API-first architecture (OpenAPI, OAuth 2.0) that deploys on NIC infrastructure and interoperates with departmental ERPs — no proprietary lock-in.

Real-Time Reporting Dashboards

Live programme-monitoring dashboards that give leadership a real-time view of operations across every unit, district or scheme.
