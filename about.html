A Decade Delivering for India’s Public Sector

Webority Technologies is a CMMI Level 5 certified software engineering and AI company headquartered in Gurugram, Haryana. Since 2012, we have partnered with Central Government ministries, paramilitary forces, national regulatory bodies and state institutions.

Our Story

Not a Company that occasionally takes a Government Project

The public sector is a core vertical for us — with a dedicated delivery team, compliance infrastructure, and institutional knowledge built across 10+ years of government engagements.

We understand RFP structures, ministry-level approval cycles, GeM procurement, and the compliance requirements that government technology demands. We don’t just deliver code — we deliver systems that are VAPT-cleared, GIGW-compliant, accessibility-tested, and SLA-backed from day one.

From Parliament of India to the Ministry of Tribal Affairs, our reference clients represent some of the most demanding, security-sensitive deployment environments in India.
